Products

Two flagship devices to improve restraint and safety during routine procedures (radiology, minor surgeries, anaesthesia…).

PetHolder

Suction cup system to stabilise a sedated animal on a smooth table.

  • Compatible surfaces: glass, metal, plastic, stainless steel (smooth non-porous surfaces).
  • Diameter: 82.6 mm • Holding force: 5 to 10 kg / cup (shear force on smooth surface).
  • Indications: radiology, minor procedures, gentle restraint under sedation.
  • Recommendation: use only on sedated animals.

FAQ

Most frequently asked questions.

On which surfaces does the PetHolder work?

On smooth, non-porous surfaces (e.g. glass, metal, plastic, stainless steel). Adhesion depends on the cleanliness and flatness of the surface.

Can it be used on a non-sedated animal?

No. The device is intended for use on sedated animals only, to avoid sudden movements.

What is the maximum table thickness for the PetAnesth clamp?

The clamp opens up to 5 cm. An adapter can be requested for thicker tables.

How can I get a technical datasheet?

Via the contact form — please specify your clinic and your need (radiology, anaesthesia…).
