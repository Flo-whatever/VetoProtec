PetHolder

Suction cup restraint system for sedated animals

PetHolder is a restraint device designed to keep sedated animals in a stable position on a smooth surface during radiological examinations, minor surgical procedures or general veterinary care.

Its high-adhesion suction cups provide reliable fixation while ensuring the safety of both the animal and the care team.
