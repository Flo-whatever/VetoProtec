PetAnesth

Anaesthesia circuit holding device for veterinary use

PetAnesth is a holding device designed for veterinary clinics and practices looking to secure and stabilise their anaesthesia circuits during intubation and surgical procedures.

It reduces traction on the endotracheal tube, improves circuit stability and optimises access to the surgical field.

Getting started

Setup in 4 steps

A simple protocol that fits naturally into your surgical routine.

1
Attach to the table Clamp the support onto the edge of the surgical table (compatible with tables up to 5 cm thick).
2
Position the articulated arm Orient the 360° arm to bring the support bracket above the surgical field.
3
Secure the circuit Insert the anaesthesia circuit into the retaining notches of the support bracket.
4
Adjust stiffness Turn the tension knob to lock the arm in the desired position.
