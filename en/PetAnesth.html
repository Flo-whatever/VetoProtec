PetAnesth

Anaesthesia circuit holder for veterinary surgery

The PetAnesth features seven lateral clip-in slots allowing multiple anaesthesia tubes to be held simultaneously.
Compatible with standard diameters used in veterinary medicine, it is equipped with a rotating bracket for freely orienting the tubing. This design minimises tension on the breathing circuit and reduces the risk of endotracheal tube displacement.

Setup

Installation in 4 steps

A simple protocol that integrates naturally into your surgical routine.

1
Attach to the tableClamp the support onto the edge of the surgical table (compatible with up to 5 cm thickness).
2
Orient the articulated armPosition the 360° articulated arm to bring the support bracket over the surgical field.
3
Route the circuitEngage the anaesthesia circuit into the holding slots of the support bracket.
4
Adjust the tensionTurn the tension dial to lock the arm in the desired position.
